Waybreak is a city management RTS with rpg elements. You begin the game by choosing a founding party to settle an outpost in no-man's-land between several large empires. As you develop the outpost you will attract more settlers and trade, but with more wealth and power you must be ready to defend what you've built from the dangers of the wilderness, bandits, foreign armies, and legendary monsters.
